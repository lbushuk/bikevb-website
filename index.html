BikeVB.ca

About

BikeVB provides bicycle repair services at cottagers' homes within the vehicle restricted area at Victoria Beach.

History

BikeVB's cottage bike repair service began as a bike repair stand parked in front of the Tennis courts at Victoria Beach. We now offer repair services at cottagers' homes with the use of a mobile bicycle repair trailer towed behind a bicycle.
